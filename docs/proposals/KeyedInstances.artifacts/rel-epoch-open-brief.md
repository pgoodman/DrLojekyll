# §9 "DeltaRel → Rel" — the epoch-open brief

## 1. CHARTER

Complete the IR into ONE relational authority for ALL flow. Today the
ControlFlow IR is generated by TWO generators over one semantic source
(§9, KeyedInstances.md:1181-1195): DR-ops (the stratum machinery + every
fold) AND a direct DataFlow walk (the hand-coded eager web + its
scaffolding), interleaved at the contract-guarded LowerIngestFold hole
seam. Rel deletes the seam: the monotone eager interior — the ORIGINAL
Push-method eager web, which today has no op kinds and is INVISIBLE to
the model (GT-5) — becomes modeled DR-IR ops lowered from the flow
graph.

DIFFERENTIALNESS BECOMES AN OP ATTRIBUTE / REGIME, not the IR's
identity — the exact direction D1's regime-split instance op family
already took (effect multisets split on DIFF:=TableIsDifferential; the
same op kind carries a monotone and a differential lowering). "DeltaRel"
the name follows suit: the DeltaRel→Rel rename rides the lib/DR→
lib/DeltaRel rename ritual precedent (MEMORY: IR observability
directives, 2026-07-18).

MOTIVATING MEASUREMENT (owner-observed at tip, §19(H):2100-2105):
`.deltarel` is 12-16 lines on MONOTONE programs (symrec_tie_1 = 12;
demand_tc_witness = 16) vs 419-427 on DIFFERENTIAL ones
(fixpoint_stress_1 = 419; average_weight = 427) — precisely because the
monotone interior has no op kinds and is invisible to the dump. The
model's proportionality is inverted: the largest emission surface casts
the smallest shadow.
